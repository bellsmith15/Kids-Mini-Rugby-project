Club offers

Group Age Sessions

  1. U6 - Multi-tasking games to learn new skills and build confidence
  2. U7 - Rugby drills and fun games, learning co-ordination for tag rugby
  3. U8 - Mini tag rugby with 5 to 6 players on each team
  4. U9 - Mini tag rugby with 6 to 7 players on each team with the addition of substitutions
  5. U10 - Mini tag rugby with 7 to 8 players on each team with rolling substitutions

About Us

Who are we?

We are a club that supports the young people in the Guildford area to play mini rugby.


We give all ages a chance to play, starting from 5 years old to 10.


Mini Rugby is played on Saturdays and Sundays.

The Coaching team

Mini Rugby is coached by the club players who give their time to provide rugby sessions for young children. Making sure that the values that they play by are passed down to the youngest in the club. During the game, players will learn how to make tricky decisions which will in turn help them to gain confidence as they learn the rules of the game. Through rugby, children can develop the capability to analyse and deduce general principles in life which can in turn boost their mental, social and physical agility.

What do we do?

We offer a friendly safe environment for young players to develop their rugby and life skills.


We help develop childrens communicaion, leadership and teamworking skills.


Improve their ball-handling and kicking skills


Opportunity to meet and make new friends in a family environment.
