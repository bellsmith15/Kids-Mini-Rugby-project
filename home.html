Mini Rugby Sessions for 5 - 10 Year Olds

Build confidence in your young boys and girls, whilst developing agility, co-ordination and fitness through training sessions.

Instill the values of Teamwork, Respect, Enjoyment, Discipline and Sportsmanship, improving their life skills from playing together.

What we offer

Left - Offers
  • Weekly sessions during the rugby season
  • Coached by players at the club who volunteer each weekend
  • Trial sessions before you commit
  • Equipment provided at the club grounds
  • Clubhouse cafe serves hot food and refreshments
Right - Age sessions
  1. U6 - Multi-tasking games to learn new skills and build confidence
  2. U7 - Rugby drills and fun games, learning co-ordination for tag rugby
  3. U8 - Mini tag rugby with 5 to 6 players on each team
  4. U9 - Mini tag rugby with 6 to 7 players on each team with the addition of substitutions
  5. U10 - Mini tag rugby with 7 to 8 players on each team with rolling substitutions
