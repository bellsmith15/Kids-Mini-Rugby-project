About Us

Who are we?

We are a club that supports the young people in the Guildford area to play mini rugby.


We give all ages a chance to play, starting from 5 years old to 10 years old.


Mini Rugby is played on Saturdays and Sundays.


Older siblings can play in our junior teams that range from U11 through to U15 then progressing to our youth team at U16.


The Coaching team

Mini Rugby is coached by volunteer club players who are RFU qualified to provide rugby sessions for young children.


Making sure that the values that they play by are passed down to the youngest in the club.


During the game, players will learn how to make tricky decisions which will in turn help them to gain confidence as they learn the rules of the game.


What do we do?

We offer a friendly safe environment for young players to develop their rugby and life skills.


We help develop childrens communicaion, leadership and teamworking skills.


Improve their ball-handling and kicking skills


Opportunity to meet and make new friends in a family environment.
